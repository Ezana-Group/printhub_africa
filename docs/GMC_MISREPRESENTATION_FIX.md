# Google Merchant Center Misrepresentation Fix

## Issue Summary
Google Merchant Center has flagged PrintHub Africa for "Misrepresentation" which prevents products from showing in Kenya. This occurs when there's a mismatch between business information in GMC and the website, or lack of transparency about business practices.

## Root Causes & Fixes

### 1. Business Information Mismatch
**Problem**: GMC business info doesn't match website
**Solution**: Update GMC business information to exactly match website

#### Required GMC Business Information:
- **Business Name**: PrintHub Africa
- **Trading Name**: PrintHub (An Ezana Group Company)
- **Address**: [Exact address from website admin settings]
- **Phone**: [Primary phone from website]
- **Email**: hello@printhub.africa
- **Website**: https://printhub.africa
- **Customer Service Email**: support@printhub.africa
- **Customer Service Phone**: [Primary phone]
- **Return Address**: [Same as business address]

### 2. Lack of Business Transparency
**Problem**: Website doesn't clearly show business ownership and practices
**Solution**: ✅ **COMPLETED** - Added transparency section to about page

### 3. Missing Trust Signals
**Problem**: No customer reviews, ratings, or certifications displayed
**Solution**: ✅ **COMPLETED** - Added trust badges and ratings to footer

### 4. Product Data Mismatch
**Problem**: Product information in feed doesn't match website
**Solution**: ✅ **COMPLETED** - Enhanced feeds with accurate seller information

## Step-by-Step GMC Fix

### Step 1: Update Business Information in GMC
1. Go to [Google Merchant Center](https://merchants.google.com/)
2. Navigate to **Settings** → **Business Information**
3. Update all fields to match website exactly:
   ```
   Business name: PrintHub Africa
   Store name: PrintHub Africa
   Address: [From admin settings - Eldoret, Kenya]
   Phone: [From admin settings]
   Email: hello@printhub.africa
   Website: https://printhub.africa
   ```

### Step 2: Update Contact Information
1. In GMC, go to **Settings** → **Contact Information**
2. Set:
   ```
   Customer service email: support@printhub.africa
   Customer service phone: [Primary phone from website]
   Return address: [Same as business address]
   ```

### Step 3: Verify Website Compliance
✅ **COMPLETED** - Website now includes:
- Clear business ownership (Ezana Group Company)
- Complete contact information
- Trust badges and ratings
- Business registration status
- SSL security indicators
- Customer service information

### Step 4: Update Product Feed
✅ **COMPLETED** - Google Merchant feed now includes:
- Accurate seller information
- Business contact details
- Proper categorization
- Kenya-specific shipping

### Step 5: Request Review
1. In GMC, go to **Diagnostic** → **Issues**
2. Find the "Misrepresentation" issue
3. Click **Request Review**
4. Provide explanation:
   ```
   We have updated our website and GMC business information to ensure complete transparency:

   1. Added comprehensive business information section to about page
   2. Updated GMC business details to match website exactly
   3. Added trust signals and customer ratings
   4. Enhanced product feeds with accurate seller information
   5. Improved business transparency and contact information

   All business information now matches between GMC and website.
   ```
